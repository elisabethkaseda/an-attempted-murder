Looking around the kitchen, your eyes settle on two things: a jar of nuts that you received as a gift and haven’t really eaten and a slightly munched up spoon that you accidentally dropped down the garbage disposal last week. A Successful Murder

You approach them and the crows scatter. Wondering whether you made the right choice, you place another handful of nuts on the ground and back away a few steps. Pretty soon, the crows have returned, bringing a few more friends with them. You feel the crows watching you, and they seem pleased with your nutty offerings.

As you watch the murder happily eating, you notice one small crow hop toward you. It drops something shiny on the ground a little bit away from your feet and looks up at you before hopping back to its group. It seems you’ve made some friends.
